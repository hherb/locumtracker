# LocumTracker - User Manual

Welcome to LocumTracker! This guide will help you get started with tracking your locum work, managing receipts, and ensuring rural subsidy compliance.

## Quick Start

### 1. First Time Setup
1. Open the app on your iOS or macOS device
2. Enter your professional details (name, ABN, GST status)
3. Set your default rates and preferences
4. Grant calendar access for conflict checking

### 2. Create Your First Assignment
1. Tap "Add Assignment"
2. Enter location and date range
3. Select rate structure (daily or hourly)
4. Set your rates and save

### 3. Record Daily Work
1. Go to "Daily" tab
2. Tap today's date
3. Confirm pre-filled location and times
4. Add sessions if needed

## Core Features

### Assignment Management

#### Creating Assignments
- **Location**: Enter workplace address or select existing location
- **MMM Classification**: Automatically detected for rural subsidy eligibility
- **Date Range**: Start and end dates for contract period
- **Rate Structure**: 
  - Daily Rate: Fixed amount per day (most common)
  - Hourly Rate: Per-hour rate with optional extras

#### Rate Options
- **Base Rate**: Your standard daily or hourly rate
- **On-Call Rate**: Additional pay for on-call availability
- **Call-Out Rate**: Pay for emergency call-outs
- **Special Rates**: Weekend, holiday, or night differentials

### Daily Recording

#### Quick Entry (Default)
For most days, simply:
1. Tap today's date
2. Confirm pre-filled information
3. Tap "Save" - Done!

#### Detailed Entry
When you need to make changes:
1. Tap "Edit Details"
2. **Location Override**: Change location if different from assignment
3. **Add Sessions**: Add multiple work periods with start/end times
4. **Travel Time**: Add travel time if >1 hour (counts toward subsidy)
5. **Notes**: Add important information

#### Session Types
- **Regular**: Standard work sessions
- **On-Call**: On-call availability periods
- **Call-Out**: Emergency call-out sessions

### Rural Subsidy Compliance

#### MMM Classifications
Your locations are automatically classified:
- **MMM1-2**: Metropolitan/Regional (no subsidy)
- **MMM3-7**: Rural/Remote (subsidy eligible)

#### Quarterly Quota Tracking
- **Target**: 40 hours across MMM3-7 locations per quarter
- **Progress**: Real-time tracking on dashboard
- **Warnings**: Alerts when you're at risk of not meeting quota
- **Deadline**: End of quarter (Mar 31, Jun 30, Sep 30, Dec 31)

#### Subsidy Calculations
- **Base Rates**: MMM4: $15/hour, MMM5: $25/hour, MMM6: $45/hour, MMM7: $65/hour
- **Vocational**: 100% rate for vocational doctors
- **Non-Vocational**: 80% rate for non-vocational doctors
- **Travel Credits**: Travel time >1 hour counts toward quota

### Receipt Management

#### Capture Receipts
- **iOS**: Use camera or photo picker
- **macOS**: Drag-and-drop files or select from Finder
- **Categories**: Travel, accommodation, meals, supplies, other
- **Linking**: Connect receipts to specific work days

#### Receipt Categories
- **Travel**: Flights, fuel, rental cars
- **Accommodation**: Hotels, temporary housing
- **Meals**: Business meals and entertainment
- **Supplies**: Medical supplies and equipment
- **Other**: Miscellaneous work expenses

### Calendar Integration

#### Conflict Detection
- **Automatic**: Checks your calendar for existing appointments
- **Warnings**: Alerts when work sessions conflict with calendar events
- **Resolution**: Helps you reschedule or confirm conflicts
- **Read-Only**: Never modifies your calendar, only reads it

#### Work Events
- **Optional**: Add work sessions to your calendar
- **Automatic**: Includes location, times, and notes
- **Reminders**: Get notifications for upcoming work

### Invoice Generation

#### Create Invoices
1. Select date range or specific assignments
2. Choose invoice template (Australian default)
3. Review calculations and GST amounts
4. Generate PDF and/or JSON export

#### Australian Features
- **ABN Validation**: Validates Australian Business Numbers
- **GST Calculations**: 10% GST for registered businesses
- **Tax Invoice**: Compliance with ATO requirements
- **B2B Support**: Customer ABN validation over $1,000

#### Export Options
- **PDF**: Professional invoices for clients
- **JSON**: Import into accounting software
- **Batch Export**: Multiple invoices at once

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Sync Problems
- **Check Internet**: CloudKit requires internet connection
- **Storage Space**: Ensure device has free space
- **Restart App**: Force quit and reopen
- **Update App**: Keep app updated

#### Calendar Access
- **Permissions**: Grant calendar access in Settings
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Calendar > LocumTracker
- **macOS**: System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Privacy > Calendar

#### Camera Access
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Camera > LocumTracker
- **Photos**: Also grant Photos access for existing images

#### MMM Classification
- **Wrong Classification**: Manually override if incorrect
- **New Locations**: Classification may take a moment to detect
- **Edge Cases**: Some locations may need manual classification

- **CloudKit Sync**: Encrypted storage and transmission
